# Desktop Goose

The internet's favorite chaos agent. A mischievous goose that terrorizes your desktop in the most delightful way.

## Features

- **Cursor stealing** — the goose grabs your mouse and drags it around
- **Mud tracks** — leaves dirty footprints across your screen
- **Angry notes** — pops up messages like "HONK" and "GOOSE WAS HERE"
- **Meme delivery** — sometimes brings you memes from the internet
- **Mod support** — community mods add new behaviors and appearances

## Why People Love It

Desktop Goose went viral for a reason. It's the perfect blend of annoying and adorable. Streamers love it for content, and developers love it for the sheer audacity of a goose that doesn't respect personal boundaries.
